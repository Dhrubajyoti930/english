Adrienne Rich at the English Party It was early January at Barton Cottage, England. Sir John Middleton had planned a party for some renowned writers, including Adrienne Rich, Pablo Neruda, Robert Frost, William Douglas, and Jane Austen. The party was held at Barton Cottage and was attended by Mrs. Dashwood and her three daughters, along with guests such as Willoughby, Mrs. John Middleton, Mrs. Middleton, and Colonel Brandon.
